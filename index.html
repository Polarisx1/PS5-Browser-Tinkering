III. Field kit

working tools built on confirmed primitives
The field kit treats the PS5 browser as a real destination, not just a subject of study. Each tool below combines confirmed-working primitives (Cache API, crypto, fetch, localStorage) into something useful you can actually run on a PS5 today.
K-01 // BRIDGE

Outbox · send

Enter the destination's pickup code, write your message, hit ENCRYPT & SEND. Encryption happens before any byte leaves this device.

Advanced — credentials, manual relay, recovery

When auto-discovery fails (PS5 WebKit may block certain relays), you can transfer credentials manually by copying the bundle below onto another device.

The bridge is symmetric: both PC and PS5 load the same page, get a pickup code, and either side can send or receive. The crypto (ECDH P-256 + AES-GCM) runs entirely in-browser. The relay handles opaque ciphertext only. If every relay we try is blocked from the PS5, the credentials bundle in advanced lets you bridge devices manually — your PC reads the pubkey off your TV, encrypts locally, and you display the result back to the PS5.
